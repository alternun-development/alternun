# Cookies Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

### Essential Cookies

- **Purpose**: Required for the website to function properly
- **Examples**: Authentication, security, load balancing
- **Duration**: Session-based or persistent as required

### Performance Cookies

- **Purpose**: Collect information about how visitors use our website
- **Examples**: Page views, time spent, navigation patterns
- **Duration**: Typically 30 days

### Functional Cookies

- **Purpose**: Remember your preferences and choices
- **Examples**: Language settings, theme preferences, customizations
- **Duration**: Varies by function (typically 1 year)

### Marketing Cookies

- **Purpose**: Deliver relevant advertisements and content
- **Examples**: Ad tracking, personalization, analytics
- **Duration**: Varies by provider (typically 90 days)

## Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type    | Purpose                     | Duration | Third Party            |
| -------------- | --------------------------- | -------- | ---------------------- |
| Authentication | Session management          | Session  | No                     |
| Analytics      | Website usage analysis      | 30 days  | Yes (Google Analytics) |
| Preferences    | User settings               | 1 year   | No                     |
| Marketing      | Advertising personalization | 90 days  | Yes                    |

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies in several ways:

### Browser Settings

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

### Cookie Consent Banner

Our website displays a cookie consent banner where you can:

- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences

### Opt-Out Links

Some third-party services provide opt-out mechanisms:

- [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)

## Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

### Analytics Services

- **Google Analytics**: Website traffic analysis
- **Hotjar**: User behavior tracking
- **Purpose**: Improve website performance and user experience

### Marketing Services

- **Google Ads**: Advertising personalization
- **Facebook Pixel**: Social media marketing
- **Purpose**: Deliver relevant advertisements

## Cookie Lifespan

- **Session Cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
- **Flash Cookies**: Stored through Adobe Flash Player (if applicable)

## Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

- Be informed about cookies we use
- Consent to non-essential cookies
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Access and delete your data
- Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your website experience:

- Some features may not work properly
- You may need to log in repeatedly
- Personalized content may not display
- Website performance may be slower

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
