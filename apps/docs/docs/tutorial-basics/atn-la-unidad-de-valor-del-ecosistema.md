# ATN — The unit of ecosystem value

How value circulates and is distributed in our ecosystem

At Alternun, impact isn't just recognized: it's organized, exchanged, and redistributed.

That's what **ATN** is for.

**ATN is the unit of value that allows the entire ecosystem to function in a coordinated and transparent way.**

It's not a technical concept or something reserved for experts.

**It's simply how Alternun represents and distributes value within the platform.**

## What is ATN?

ATN is a **digital asset of the Alternun ecosystem** used for:

- **Receiving rewards**
- **Participating in projects**
- **Accessing benefits**
- **Exchanging value** for services, advantages, or, if you prefer, money

Think of ATN as **the points in a rewards program**, but with a crucial difference:

**ATN has real value within the system and can be used in multiple ways.**

## What is ATN for?

ATN serves several essential roles:

- **It's how** [Regenerative Basic Income (RBI)](/docs/tutorial-basics/rbi-regenerative-basic-income) is paid
- **It allows you to participate** in projects and activities within the ecosystem
- **It's used to access** discounts, preferential conditions, and exclusive opportunities
- **It acts as the vehicle** through which Alternun redistributes value generated by regenerative activities

**ATN doesn't exist to be accumulated without purpose.**

**It exists to circulate, coordinate, and strengthen the ecosystem.**

## How do you get ATN?

ATN is obtained in various ways within Alternun:

- **As a result of RBI**, based on your [AIRS](/docs/tutorial-basics/airs-tu-huella-regenerativa)
- **Through rewards** for participating in the ecosystem
- **Through exchanges** within the platform
- **By participating** in specific initiatives or projects

In all cases, **ATN arrives as a consequence of clear action or contribution.**

ATN can also be purchased so you can complete actions within the platform.

## ATN and ecosystem balance

An important part of ATN's design is that **its quantity is managed responsibly.**

Each time the ecosystem generates value, a portion of that value is used to **progressively reduce the amount of ATN in circulation**, strengthening the system's balance and benefiting all participants.

**This ensures that ATN is not just an accumulation point, but a unit of value that reflects the health of the ecosystem.**

## What can you do with your ATN?

With ATN you can:

- **Access benefits** within Alternun
- **Participate in regenerative projects**
- **Get better conditions** on the platform
- **Convert value into rewards or income** in dollars, according to your preferences

**ATN is flexible by design:**

It can be used, exchanged, or held, always within clear and transparent rules.

## ATN as a bridge

ATN **connects two worlds:**

- **The world of everyday actions**, commitment, and participation
- **The world of value redistribution**, incentives, and benefits

**Through ATN, Alternun can function as a system where impact generates value and value returns to people.**
