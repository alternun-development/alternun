# AIRS — Your regenerative footprint

Your reputation and participation in the ecosystem

At Alternun, we don't believe that impact is measured only in money.

We believe that **true value lies in what you do, how you participate, and how you sustain the ecosystem over time.**

That's why we created **AIRS**.

## What is AIRS?

AIRS is **Alternun's reputation and participation system.**

It's a **score that reflects your real contribution** to the regenerative ecosystem.

Every action you take within Alternun—compensating, supporting projects, maintaining regenerative assets, participating in initiatives—adds AIRS to your account.

**Unlike other systems, AIRS cannot be bought.**

It's built over time, with verifiable actions and commitment.

## What is AIRS for?

AIRS is the foundation that connects the entire ecosystem:

- **Defines your status** within Alternun
- **Determines your participation** in [Regenerative Basic Income (RBI)](/docs/tutorial-basics/rbi-regenerative-basic-income)
- **Gives you access** to benefits, discounts, and opportunities within the platform
- **Builds your regenerative reputation**, visible and verifiable

**In short: AIRS is how Alternun recognizes those who make the system work.**

## How do you get AIRS?

AIRS is earned by **actively participating in the ecosystem.**

Some of the most common ways include:

- **Making verified compensations**
- **Supporting regenerative projects** by buying or holding eligible tokens
- **Participating in initiatives, campaigns, or actions** promoted by Alternun and its partners
- **Maintaining sustained and responsible activity** within the platform

Each action has **a defined and transparent AIRS value.**

**There are no shortcuts or artificial accumulations.**

## AIRS grows with you

Your AIRS score **always grows.**

**It's not spent, not burned, not transferred.**

Over time, AIRS allows you to reach different statuses within the ecosystem—from initial levels to the most advanced—that reflect your track record and consistency.

**Status is not just a symbol: it enables real benefits and greater participation in the value Alternun generates.**

## AIRS and time

AIRS is **designed to reward consistency, not speculation.**

It's not just about what you do once, but **how you keep participating.**

That's why the system takes into account **sustained activity and consistency over time.**

This approach makes AIRS fair for both those starting today and those who've supported the ecosystem for years.

## People and organizations

AIRS can be accumulated by both **individuals and organizations.**

In both cases, AIRS functions as **a regenerative reputation metric:**

A clear signal of commitment, traceable and comparable within the ecosystem.

This opens the door to new forms of collaboration between individuals, companies, and projects with aligned values.

## AIRS is not just a number

**AIRS is an impact narrative.**

It's the story of how you participate in regeneration and how that participation translates into shared value.

**At Alternun, your impact matters, and AIRS is how it becomes visible.**
